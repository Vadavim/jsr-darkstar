TRAIT CHANGES

ABILITY CHANGES

[Azure Lore]
 - Lasts for 2 minutes and instead does the following:
 	- Doubles System Bonus / Penalty to Blue Spells
	- Instantly resets Chain and Burst Affinity. While under Azure Lore, their recast times are halved.
	- +40 Accuracy / Magic Accuracy with Spells
        - Certain spells have special effects while under Azure Lore
        

[Burst Affinity]
 - Increases potency of next magical blue spell based on current TP.
 - On a successful magic burst, returns 33% of MP spent.

[Efflux]
 - Gained at level 60
 - Instead resets Burst Affinity and Chain Affinity.

[Convergence
 - Gained at level 30
 - Recast is 8 minutes
 - Halves casting time when of AoE spells and reduces cost by 33% in addition to normal effect.

[Unbridled Wisdom]
 - Gained at level 50
 - Recast: 5 minutes
 - Refunds MP of next Blue Magic spell, or until one minute has passed

MAGIC CHANGES

- Family Bonus:
 - When using a spell against a family that is weak against the spell's family, the spell does +15% damage and has +20 accuracy. 
 - Likewise, when using a spell against a family that is strong against the spell's family, the spell does -15% damage and has -20 accuracy.
	- The exception are Dragons, Undead, Arcana, and Demons. Such spells receive the penalty if the family is the same as the spell's family.
 - Beastmen are weak against beastmen spells.
