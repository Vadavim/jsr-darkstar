TRAIT CHANGES

[Killer Traits]
 - All killer traits are gained at level 10. They start with an activation rate of 10%.
 - At levels 20, 30, 40, 50, 60, and 70, activation rate increased by 2%.

[Max HP Boost]
 - Gains a Max HP boost at levels 15, 30, 50, and 70
