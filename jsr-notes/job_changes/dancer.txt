SKILL CHANGES

TRAIT CHANGES
 - Gains Auto-Regain at levels 20, 40, and 60. Regains 5/10/15 TP per 3 seconds.


ABILITY CHANGES

[Presto]
 - Gained at level 45

[Steps]
 - Steps only require 5% TP

[Grand Pas]
 - Gained at level 65
 - Instead consumes all TP to give Regain effect to self over three minutes.


SAMBA CHANGES
[Drain Sambas]
 - Drains samba also deals damage based on amount drained.
 - Duration increased to 3 minutes.
 
[Aspir Sambas]
 - Aspir samba drains MP whether or not mob as MP.
 - Only requires 10% TP.
 - Duration increased to 3 minutes.

[Haste Samba]
 - Duration increased to 3 minutes. 

FLOURISH CHANGES

[Desperate Flourish]
 - Much higher chance to weigh down target now.
 - Also inflicts -10 EVA.
 - Lasts for 60 seconds.

[Violent Flourish]
 - Gained at Level 35
 - Stun duration increased. Much higher success rate.
 - Damage of attack increased.

[Animated Flourish]
 - Recast reduced to 20 seconds.

FLOURISHES II CHANGES

[Reverse Flourish]
 - No longer shares recast with other flourishes, but recast increased to 4 minutes.
 - TP return also increased greatly.

[Building Flourish]
 - Duration increased to 10 minutes.
 - Next Weaponskill gets a +25/+50/+75 TP bonus in addition to other effects.
