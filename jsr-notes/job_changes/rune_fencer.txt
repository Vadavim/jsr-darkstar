ABILITY CHANGES

[Rune Enchaintment]
 - Each rune also grants +10 MACC and +10 MATK with the element it is associated with.


[Elemental Sforzo]

 - Instead grants the following effects if you have at least one rune of the following.
	- Unda: Restores 100% Maximum HP. 
	- Ignis: +25% Double Attack for 2 minutes.
	- Tellus: -25% physical damage received for 2 minutes.
	- Flabra: 4 Shadows
	- Sulpor: +25% Critical Hit Chance for 2 minutes.
	- Gelus: -50% magical damage received for 2 minutes.
	- Lux: Regains 5% TP per 3 seconds for 2 minutes.
	- Tenebrae: Restores 100% Maximum MP.

[Vivacious Pulse]
 - Base healing is 15% Maximum HP.
 - Unless otherwise noted, additional effects last one minute.
 - Unda runes give regeneration (scales with level and number of runes)
 - Ignis runes give +12/15/18% Attack bonus
 - Tellus runes give +20/25/30% Defense bonus
 - Flabra runes give +20/25/30 Evasion
 - Sulppor runes give a +6/+7/+8% critical hit chance increase
 - Gelus runes give a +20/25/30 Mdef bonus.
 - Lux runes give 15/20/25 TP regen per 3 seconds.
 - Tenebrae runes restore MP (scales with level and number of runes)

[Swordplay]
 - Instead lasts until HP reaches 50% or lower.
 - Duration increase to 3 minutes.

[Batuta]
 - Gained at level 30.
 - Flabra runes gives +10 Parrying per rune
 - Sulpor / Unda / Lux runes give +7 Parrying per rune
 - The other runes give +5 Parrying per rune.

[Rayke]
 - Gained at 35
 - Recast reduced to 180 seconds.
 - Deals elemental damage based on runes.
 - Has a chance to inflict status effects based on runes. Chance increased by additional runes of same element.
	
	- Ignis: Attack -10% for one minute.
	- Unda: (5 + level / 2)/tic Poison for one minute.
	- Tellus: Defense -10% for one minute.
	- Flabra: Evasion -20 for one minute.
	- Sulpor: Accuracy -20 for one minute.
	- Gelus: MACC and MATK -10% for one minute.
	- Lux: -100 TP
	- Tenebrae: Dispels one effect


[Embolden]
 - Reduced recast to 3 minutes.


WARD CHANGES

[Pflug]

[Liement]

SPELL ENHANCEMENTS
 - Harboring at least one rune of the associated element grants the enhanced effect.


[Aquaveil]
 - With an Unda rune, increases spell interruption reduction to -80% and reduces fire damage by 25%.

[Protect Spells]
 - With a Tellus rune, increases Defense bonus for self by 50%.

[Shell Spells]
 - With a Gelus rune, increases M.Defense bonus for self by 50%.

[Regen / Regen II]
 - With a Lux rune, increases regeneration by 50%.
 - With an Unda rune, increases duration by 50%.
 - With a Tenebrae rune, reduces duration by 50%.

[Blink]
 - With a Flabra rune, grants 2 additional shadows.

[Blaze Spikes]
 - With an Ignis rune, greatly increases fire damage.
 - With a Tenebrae rune, doubles duration.

[Ice Spikes]
 - With a Gelus rune, moderately increses ice damage.
 - With a Tenebrae rune, doubles duration.

[Flash]
 - With a Lux rune, increases duration by 2 seconds.
 - With a Sulpor rune, increases duration by 2 seconds.
 - With an Ignis rune, increased enmity generation.
 - With a Tenebrae rune, enmity is instead reduced considerably.

[Refresh]
 - With a Lux rune, increases duration by 30 seconds.
 - With a Tenebrae rune, increases Refresh by 1.

[Phalanx]
 - With a Lux rune, increases duration by 50%.
 - With a Tellus rune, increases damage reduction by 50%.

[Stoneskin]
 - With a Tellus rune, increases amount absorbed by 50%.

[Foil]
 - With a Flabra rune, grants self 10% Haste (stacks with normal haste).
 - With a Sulpor rune, grants self 5% Double Attack.
