[Acquiring Orbs]
 - You can sometimes get orbs through quests and other means.
 - Orbs are no longer Rare (although they will appear as such in-game).
 - If you lose a battlefield, your orb will be refunded to you.

[Battlefield Rewards]
 - You are also rewarded experience points upon completing a battlefield.
 - When you win a battlefield, and you were the one who traded the orb, you are rewarded one
   augmented item from a pool of items depending upon the battlefield.
