[Power Slash]
 - On a critical hit, gain +15% Attack bonus for 60 seconds.

[Frost Bite]
 - On a hit, applies Frost. Duration varies with TP (30 at 1000 to 90 at 3000).

[Freezebite]
 - Applies Paralyze (potency is 25%). Duration varies with TP (30 at 100 to 90 at 3000).

[Crescent Moon]
 - Damage increased by 25% during Waxing/Waning Crescent moon phase.

[Herculean Slash]
 - Gained at skill level 260
 - Instead inflicts Slow (35%). Duration varies with TP (60 at 1000 to 180 at 3000).
