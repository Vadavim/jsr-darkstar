[Heavy Swing]
 - Attack Multiplier: 1.0 -> 1.5

[Rock Crusher]
 - On a hit, inflicts Rasp. Duration varies with TP (30 at 1000 to 240 at 3000).
 - KI:
	- Ki increases Rasp's DoT power

[Earth Crusher]
 - Modifiers increased to 60% Str and 40% Int
 - fTP: 1.35 (1000), 2.5 (2000), 4.5 (3000)
 - Grants Stoneskin for 10 minutes. 
	- Base Power: 5 + level * 2.5
	- +1% power per 10 TP above 1000.

 - KI: increases potency of Stoneskin

[Starburst]
 - Element is either Light or Dark, whichever the target is weak to. Otherwise it's random.
 - Modifiers are 50% STR and 50% MND
 - Does not increase enmity
 - Attempts to dispel a status effect (Light or Dark based, whichever creature is less resistant to)

[Sunburst]
  - Element is either Light or Dark, whichever the target is weak to. Otherwise it's random.
  - Modifiers are 150% MND
  - +33% damage against demons and undead.
  - Heals HP equal to 50% of the damage dealt.
  - KI:
	- Increases amount of HP healed
	- At 250+ Ki, heals whole party

[Shell Crusher]
 - Ignores Defense based on TP: 30% (1000), 50% (2000), 70% (3000)
 - +33% damage against Aquans.

[Full Swing]
 - fTP is 2 (1000), 4 (2000), 7.5 (3000).
 - Attack is more accurate (+40 accuracy).

[Spirit Taker]
 - Mods are 80% Int and 80% Mnd
 - KI: if Boost is active, increases Ki by TP / 5

[Retribution]
 - The target receives damage the next three times that it damages you or an ally.
	- For every 500 additional TP, effect occurs one additional time
 - KI:
	- Grants Retaliation for (KI / 3) seconds.

[Cataclysm]
 - Gained at skill level 250
 - Modifiers: 50% INT, 50% STR
 - fTP: 3 (1000), 6 (2000), 9 (3000)
 - Does not cause enmity.

[Shattersoul]
 - Gained at skill level 270
 - Lowers Magic Defense by 15
