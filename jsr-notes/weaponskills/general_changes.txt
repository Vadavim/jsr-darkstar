[Magic Weaponskills]
 - All magic weaponskills have an inherent +20 bonus to magic accuracy.
 - Base damage for magic weaponskills increased (1.5 per level)
 - They gain an additional +1 bonus to MACC for every 50 TP above 1000.
 - Magic weaponskills are more affected by weather and day bonuses matching the element of the skill.
 - Magic weaponskills are more affected by elemental attack and defense mods
