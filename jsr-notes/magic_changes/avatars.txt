GENERAL
 - WARD buff durations affected by summoning skill mod and CHR mod.
 - CHR influences avatar's damage, accuracy, and other effects
 - Not all RAGE / WARD abilities have the same recast time
 - Physcal RAGE bloodpacts do not spend TP (and instead give a bonus).
 - A- Weapon Skill
 - C+ Evasion and Defense


AVATAR's FAVOR
 - Avatar's favor is automatically given to all party members when avatar is summoned.
 - Gives the following bonuses based on the avatar's element:
	- -10% ELEMENT damage received
	- +10% ELEMENT attack and accuracy
	- +40 ELEMENT resistance
 - Also gives special bonus depending upon avatar. May be different than retail.
